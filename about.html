About Sapida Lalehgan Trading Company

Sapida Lalehgan Trading Company is a Kabul-based import and export company specializing in pharmaceutical and medical products for the Afghan market.

We operate as a trade intermediary and market access partner, connecting reliable international manufacturers with established distribution channels across Afghanistan. The company does not engage in manufacturing; its core role is to facilitate structured, transparent, and market-informed trade.

Our strength lies in product sourcing, supplier coordination, and distribution support, based on direct, hands-on experience within Afghanistan’s pharmaceutical wholesale and retail sectors.

Our Vision

To become a trusted local trade partner for international pharmaceutical and medical product manufacturers seeking reliable and transparent access to the Afghan market.

Our Mission

To provide dependable import and export solutions by sourcing quality pharmaceutical and medical products aligned with verified Afghan market demand, ensuring competitive pricing, timely coordination, and professional communication.

Market Coverage & Business Model

Primary Market: Kabul and Northern Provinces of Afghanistan

Business Model: Import, export, and trade representation

Target Clients: Wholesalers, distributors, pharmacies, retailers, and institutions

Ownership & Market Experience

The company is owned and managed by a professional with extensive experience in pharmaceutical retail and wholesale distribution in Afghanistan.

The owner currently operates an active wholesale pharmacy, providing real-time market insight and established distribution relationships.

Compliance & Trade Standards
