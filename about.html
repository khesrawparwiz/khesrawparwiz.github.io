About Sapida Lalehgan Trading Company

Sapida Lalehgan Trading Company is an Afghanistan-based import and export company focused on pharmaceutical and medical products. The company operates as a trade intermediary and market-access partner, facilitating structured and transparent commercial relationships between international manufacturers and Afghan distribution channels.

The company does not engage in manufacturing activities. Its core mandate is to identify reliable suppliers, coordinate product sourcing, and support market entry and distribution in line with local regulatory and commercial realities.

Vision

To become a trusted and long-term trade partner for international pharmaceutical and medical product manufacturers seeking dependable access to the Afghan market.

Mission

To provide professional import and export solutions by sourcing quality-assured pharmaceutical and medical products that align with verified Afghan market demand, while ensuring transparency, reliability, and efficient coordination.

Core Values

  • Integrity and ethical business conduct
  • Transparency in trade operations
  • Reliability and accountability
  • Quality-focused sourcing
  • Long-term partnership orientation

Business Activities

  • Import of pharmaceutical products
  • Import of medical devices and supplies
  • Export of selected Afghan-origin products
  • International supplier representation
  • Trade facilitation and coordination
  • Distribution and market support services

Market Coverage & Business Model

Primary Market: Kabul and Northern provinces of Afghanistan

Business Model: Import, export, and trade representation

Target Clients: Wholesalers, distributors, pharmacies, healthcare facilities, NGOs, and institutions

Ownership & Market Experience

The company is owned and managed by a professional with extensive hands-on experience in pharmaceutical wholesale and retail distribution in Afghanistan.

The owner currently operates an active wholesale pharmacy, providing direct access to market intelligence, pricing dynamics, and established distribution networks.

Why Choose Sapida Lalehgan Trading Company

  • Deep understanding of Afghan market conditions
  • Direct access to wholesale and retail distribution channels
  • Transparent and compliant trade practices
  • Flexible cooperation and representation models
  • Commitment to sustainable, long-term partnerships

Compliance & Quality Standards

Products are sourced from manufacturers complying with applicable quality and safety standards, including Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) where relevant, and aligned with Afghan Ministry of Public Health regulatory requirements.

Future Outlook

The company aims to expand its international supplier network across Türkiye, India, China, and Europe, diversify product portfolios, and strengthen nationwide distribution capabilities within Afghanistan.
