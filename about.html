About Sapida Lalehgan Trading Company

Sapida Lalehgan Trading Company is a Kabul-based import and export company specializing in pharmaceutical and medical products for the Afghan market.

We operate as a trade intermediary and market access partner, connecting reliable international manufacturers with established distribution channels across Afghanistan. The company does not engage in manufacturing; its core role is to facilitate structured, transparent, and market-informed trade.
